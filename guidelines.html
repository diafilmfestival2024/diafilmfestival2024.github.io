Guidelines

Submission Guidelines:

Films must be submitted as 1920x1080p to YouTube.

Films should be no longer than 3 minutes maximum.

Submissions must be received by the deadline (17th May 2024) in order to be considered.

All submissions must be accompanied by a brief synopsis and a list of credits.

Submissions must be the original work of the participant.

Film must be respectful to the UAE laws.

Must not harm the environment or animals.

The film must not contain any inappropriate, no explicit language, music, or graphic violence.

Must submit a 15 sec trailer of the film.

Must submit a passport size picture with white background

Top 5 finalists for each category will be announced by email.
